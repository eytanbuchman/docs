# Welcome to Kernel MKII

**Your Intelligent Content Management Platform**

Kernel MKII is a powerful content intelligence platform designed to help marketing teams organize, analyze, and extract valuable insights from their marketing materials. Transform your content chaos into organized, actionable intelligence.

![Kernel MKII Dashboard](/images/dashboard-overview.png)

## What is Kernel MKII?

Kernel MKII is an AI-powered content management system that automatically analyzes your marketing materials to:

- **Organize content** with intelligent taxonomy and tagging
- **Extract insights** like customer testimonials and key data points
- **Enable discovery** through powerful search and filtering
- **Facilitate collaboration** with team-based content management
- **Provide intelligence** with AI-powered content analysis

## Key Features

### 🚀 **Intelligent Content Upload**
Upload files or import content directly from URLs. Our AI automatically analyzes and suggests appropriate tags and classifications.

### 🏷️ **Smart Taxonomy System**
Three-tier taxonomy system (core, optional, custom fields) ensures consistent content organization across your entire library.

### 🔍 **Powerful Search & Discovery**
Find exactly what you need with advanced filtering, full-text search, and AI-powered content recommendations.

### 💎 **Automatic Insight Extraction**
AI automatically identifies and extracts customer testimonials, key data points, and valuable insights from your content.

### 👥 **Team Collaboration**
Organize content by teams, assign roles, and collaborate effectively on content strategy and management.

### 📊 **Content Intelligence**
Get actionable insights about your content performance, usage patterns, and content gaps.

## Who Uses Kernel MKII?

- **Marketing Teams** organizing campaigns and collateral
- **Content Managers** maintaining large content libraries  
- **Sales Teams** finding relevant materials quickly
- **Product Marketers** tracking competitive intelligence
- **Business Leaders** extracting insights from market research
