## Getting the Best Support Experience

### Before You Contact Us

1. **Check Documentation First**
   - Review relevant user guides and FAQs
   - Try the troubleshooting steps for your specific issue
   - Search our knowledge base for similar problems

2. **Gather Information**
   - Note the exact error message (if any)
   - Take screenshots of the issue
   - Identify when the problem started
   - Try to reproduce the issue

3. **Have Account Details Ready**
   - Your workspace name
   - Your user role (admin, member, etc.)
   - Browser type and version
   - Operating system information

### Information to Include in Your Support Request

#### **For All Issues**
- **Clear Subject Line**: Briefly describe the problem
- **Detailed Description**: Step-by-step explanation of what you were doing
- **Expected vs. Actual**: What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened
- **Workspace Information**: Your workspace name and your role
- **Browser & OS**: Browser type/version and operating system

#### **For Technical Issues**
- **Error Messages**: Exact text of any error messages
- **Screenshots**: Visual evidence of the problem
- **Steps to Reproduce**: Detailed steps to recreate the issue
- **Timing**: When did the issue first occur?
- **Impact**: How is this affecting your work?

#### **For Feature Requests**
- **Use Case**: Why you need this feature
- **Current Workaround**: How you're handling this now
- **Business Impact**: How this would improve your workflow
- **Priority Level**: How important this is to your organization

#### **For Account Issues**
- **Account Email**: The email address associated with your account
- **Workspace Name**: Name of the affected workspace
- **User Role**: Your current role and permissions
- **Change Requested**: Specific changes needed

## What Happens After You Contact Us

### 1. **Acknowledgment**
You'll receive an automatic confirmation that we've received your request, along with a ticket number for tracking.

### 2. **Initial Response**
A support team member will respond within our committed timeframe with either:
- A solution to your problem
- Questions to better understand the issue
- An update on investigation progress

### 3. **Resolution Process**
We'll work with you to:
- Diagnose the root cause
- Provide step-by-step solutions
- Test the resolution with you
- Ensure the issue is fully resolved

### 4. **Follow-up**
After resolution, we'll:
- Confirm the issue is resolved
- Ask for feedback on the support experience
- Update our knowledge base if needed
- Schedule follow-up if necessary
