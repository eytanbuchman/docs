# Getting Started with Kernel MKII

Welcome to Kernel MKII! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get up and running with your new content intelligence platform.

## Account Setup

### Creating Your Account

1. **Sign Up**: Visit the Kernel MKII platform and create your account
2. **Email Verification**: Check your email and verify your account
3. **Initial Setup**: Complete the onboarding process

![Account Setup Process](/images/account-setup.png)

### Setting Up Your Workspace

Your workspace is where all your content and team collaboration happens:

1. **Workspace Name**: Choose a descriptive name (e.g., "Acme Marketing Team")
2. **Organization Type**: Select your organization size and type
3. **Initial Settings**: Configure basic preferences

## First Upload

### Uploading Your First Content

Let's get some content into your system:

1. **Navigate to Assets**: Click "Assets" in the main navigation
2. **Click Upload**: Use the blue "Upload Content" button
3. **Choose Your Method**:
   - **File Upload**: Drag and drop or browse for files
   - **URL Import**: Paste a URL to import web content

![Content Upload Interface](/images/upload-modal.png)

### Supported File Types

Kernel MKII supports a wide variety of content types:

- **Documents**: PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel
- **Images**: JPG, PNG, GIF, SVG
- **Web Content**: Import directly from URLs
- **Text Files**: TXT, CSV, and more

### Tagging Your Content

During upload, you'll see the tagging interface:

1. **Content Type**: Select what type of content this is (Blog Post, White Paper, Case Study, etc.)
2. **Funnel Stage**: Where does this fit in your marketing funnel?
3. **Teams**: Assign to specific teams if applicable
4. **AI Suggestions**: Review and accept AI-powered tag suggestions

![Tagging Interface](/images/tagging-modal.png)

## Exploring the Dashboard

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your content library:

- **Recent Activity**: See your latest uploads and updates
- **Quick Stats**: Total assets, testimonials, and data points
- **Pending Reviews**: Content waiting for approval
- **Quick Actions**: Upload, search, and manage content

![Dashboard Overview](/images/dashboard-main.png)

### Navigation Menu

The main navigation includes:

- **Dashboard**: Your home base
- **Assets**: Browse and manage all content
- **Search**: Powerful search across all content
- **Testimonials**: Customer testimonials extracted from content
- **Data Points**: Key statistics and insights
- **Settings**: Account and workspace management

## Basic Tasks

### Uploading Content

**Method 1: File Upload**
1. Click "Upload Content" 
2. Drag files or click to browse
3. Fill in the tagging form
4. Click "Upload & Tag"

**Method 2: URL Import**
1. Click "Upload Content"
2. Switch to "Import from URL" tab
3. Paste the URL
4. Review pre-populated information
5. Adjust tags as needed
6. Click "Import & Tag"

### Finding Content

**Quick Search**
- Use the search bar at the top of any page
- Search by title, content, or tags

**Advanced Filtering**
1. Go to the Assets page
2. Use the filter panel on the left
3. Filter by content type, date, teams, or custom fields
4. Save frequently used filters as "Saved Views"

![Search and Filter Interface](/images/search-filters.png)

### Organizing with Teams

If you have multiple teams:

1. **Create Teams**: Go to Settings > Teams
2. **Add Members**: Invite users to specific teams
3. **Assign Content**: Tag content with relevant teams during upload
4. **Filter by Team**: Use team filters to see only relevant content

## AI-Powered Features

### Automatic Content Analysis

When you upload content, Kernel MKII automatically:

- **Extracts Text**: Pulls readable text from documents and web pages
- **Suggests Tags**: Recommends appropriate taxonomy values
- **Identifies Insights**: Finds testimonials and key data points
- **Analyzes Content**: Determines content type and funnel stage

### Smart Suggestions

Look for the "AI Suggest" buttons throughout the platform:

- **During Upload**: Get tag suggestions based on content analysis
- **In Search**: Receive related content recommendations  
- **For Organization**: Automatic categorization suggestions

![AI Suggestions Interface](/images/ai-suggestions.png)

## Common First-Time Questions

**Q: How much content should I upload initially?**
A: Start with 10-20 key pieces to get familiar with the system, then expand from there.

**Q: Can I change tags after uploading?**
A: Yes! You can edit tags and metadata anytime by clicking the edit button on any asset.

**Q: What happens to my content during processing?**
A: Content goes through automated text extraction and AI analysis. You'll see status updates as it processes.

**Q: How do I invite team members?**
A: Go to Settings > Users and click "Invite User" to send email invitations.
